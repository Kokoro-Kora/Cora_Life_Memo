My Life Memo

This is my life for the past 17 years

My Childhood

As a child, I was plagued with health problems like Asthema and Heart-Related issues. Although my health was not good, I was able to excel in class and made friends with many people, some of which are here at paragon with me but in different departments. Life in Preah Sihanouk Province back then was chill, everybody knew each other and we are all family living in a small community.

Teenager Life

During my teenage years (yes I'm still a teenager), I was addicted to video games (Vainglory). My grades has suffered tremendously for a year and half. Thanks to my parents and a particular person, I was able to gain control of my life and studied as hard as I could, resulting in good grades for my diploma exam and still dominating leaderboards in my favorite video game. If I could turn back time, I would go back to this period, as my mental health, grades , and social life was all balanced, making me the happiest person alive.

Adult Life

Although I'm still a teenager, I considered myself as an adult the moment I left home to pursue my dreams in Phnom Penh. Transitioning to the city life was hard at first, as I am not that fluent with english, technology usage and self-control. But over time, I started to feel more comfortable living alone and learned to do many things on my own like cooking, cleaning, working and finding tutor class for myself, which my parents used to do for me. After 2 years of living in a new place, I graduated from High School and pursued a higher education at Paragon Int. University. So far, this three year period gave me a taste of all flavors in life. I would definitely recommend everyone to leave home and try living alone for a while. It will change your perspective in life.

Future Goals

My future goals is very simple. My goal is to be happy and make everyone around me happy as well. It sounds simple, but it is quite hard to do. By making everyone happy, I mean that I would help others as much as I can by providing service for income, helping others , sharing my life experiences, and contribute to society in donations to Charities, Paying tax, and being a responsible citizen in society. When everybody around me is happy, I am happy too.
